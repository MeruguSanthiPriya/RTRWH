Why Use Government Portals?

  • Reliability & Verification: Workers registered on these portals have their credentials verified, reducing risks.
  • Transparency: Access a national database of workers, ensuring fair wage and skill discovery.
  • Subsidy Compliance: Hiring registered workers may be a prerequisite for certain government subsidies or loans.
  • Direct Connection: Engage directly with skilled labor without intermediaries, ensuring better communication and cost management.

e-Shram Portal

The National Database of Unorganised Workers. A primary resource for finding a wide range of gig workers, including those in construction, plumbing, and general labor suitable for RWH projects.

State BOCW Boards

Each state maintains a Building and Other Construction Workers (BOCW) Welfare Board. These portals are excellent for finding specialized construction workers and teams who are formally registered and eligible for state welfare schemes.

Find Your State's BOCW Board

National Career Service (NCS)

A comprehensive job-matching portal by the Ministry of Labour & Employment. You can post job requirements for RWH installation and find skilled professionals, from plumbers to civil engineers, seeking employment.
